OUR STORY

A Legacy of Luxury

For over 25 years, The Anantara Grand has defined the standard of hospitality in India.

WHO WE ARE
More Than a Resort

Founded in 1999, The Anantara Grand began as a vision to create India's most distinguished resort and banquet destination. Today, it stands as an iconic landmark blending Mughal grandeur with contemporary luxury.

Every corner of our property tells a story — from hand-carved pillars to curated art collections sourced from master craftsmen across India.

1999 — Founding

The Anantara Grand opens its doors with 50 rooms

2007 — Grand Expansion
